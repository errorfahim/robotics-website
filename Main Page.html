Introduction

The word is small, but its meaning is profound. Its a Japanese concept that originated in a village on the Okinawa Islands. The word is IKIGAI, Which means "the value of life." In Japanese,"iki" means life, and "gai" means means value or worth. Combined, it represents the purpose or reason for living- a concept that inspires you daily.

This new 'bot brings together fine artistry with the highest forms of modern technology in education. Mechamaru, a robot companion specifically created to assist visually impaired children with their educational challenges. By incorporating advanced technology and key features inspired by Mechamaru's cartoon counterpart, our goal is to revolutionize the field of education and empower individuals with sight problems to thrive in academic settings.
