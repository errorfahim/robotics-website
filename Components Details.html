Hardware Components

Microcontroller

Raspberry Pi 4B

A versatile, low-cost computer enabling various DIY projects, education, and IoT applications. Equipped with powerful processing capabilities, it can handle complex tasks, such as data processing, communication, and integration with sensors. Ideal for controlling advanced features in robotics and automation.

Microcontroller

Wifi Module

Main controller for managing Bluetooth (BLE) and Wi-Fi communication, facilitating the robot's navigation and interaction.Used as Bluetooth mesh nodes (beacons) for precise localization near beds and room entrances.

Sensor

Ultrasonic Sensor

HC-SR04 or similar; emit ultrasonic waves to detect obstacles by measuring the time for waves to bounce back, ensuring safe navigation.

Wheel Encoder

Wheel

Measure the distance travelled by the robot, providing real-time feedback for precise movement control.

Motors

DC Motors

Drive the robots wheels, enabling movement in multiple directions.

Motor Driver

BTS Motors Driver

controls motor speed and direction, ensuring efficient motor management.

Regulator

Voltage Regulator

Ensure stable power for microcontrollers, sensors, and other electronics.

Battery

Rechargable Battery Pack

Powers the robot and all components, offering a sustainable energy source.
